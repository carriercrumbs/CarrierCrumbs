Meet The Founder: Luis Vazquez

Luis Vazquez, raised in south Jersey, began his Videography career in his early college years. While attending school for Graphic design, he created Vlogs and recorded local events with no intentions on making music videos.

It was until he and a friend took a trip to Boston, where he met artist AG Nero in a Chicken Over Rice fast food restaurant. AG Nero then asked if Luis did Music Videos, for he saw Luis' camera equipment with him. Luis Knowing he had never shot a music video replied , "Yes, I do!" He then created his first official music video titled "No Movie by Ag Nero X Trey Da Trillest."

Now Luis having created his own video production company, Carrier Crumbs, he has new goals to lead a company that supports undervalued artists.
